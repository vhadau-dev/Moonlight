🛠️ Moonlight Haven Support Center

📌 Overview

The Moonlight Haven Support Center is designed to assist members with questions, issues, suggestions, and general guidance. Whether you are a new member or an active participant, this page will help you understand how to get help efficiently.

Support is handled through our community platforms where staff and members can assist you in real time.

💬 How to Get Support

If you encounter any issue, follow these steps:

  • Clearly describe your issue or question
  • Include screenshots if available
  • Mention relevant details (commands used, errors, etc.)
  • Contact support through Discord or WhatsApp
Join Discord Support Join WhatsApp Support

⚠️ Common Issues

  • Bot commands not responding
  • Missing Moonlight Coins (MC)
  • Shop items not appearing
  • Invite rewards not credited
  • Permission or access issues

If you experience any of these, provide full details when contacting support so the team can assist you faster.

📨 Suggestions & Feedback

Moonlight Haven is constantly improving. We welcome suggestions that help improve the system, community, and overall user experience.

You can submit ideas through Discord or WhatsApp. Constructive feedback is always appreciated.

📜 Support Guidelines

  • Be respectful to staff and members
  • Avoid spamming repeated messages
  • Do not misuse the support system
  • Provide clear and accurate information
  • Follow instructions given by support staff

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are Moonlight Coins (MC)?
MC is the official currency used within Moonlight Haven to purchase services and rewards.
How do I earn MC?
You can earn MC through invites, events, giveaways, and community participation.
Why didn’t I receive my reward?
Ensure you completed the task correctly and submitted valid proof. If issues persist, contact support with details.
Where do I claim shop items?
Shop items are processed through the bot system. Follow instructions provided after selecting an item.

⏱️ Response Time

Response times may vary depending on activity. Support teams and community members will assist you as soon as possible.
