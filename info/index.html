ℹ️ Moonlight Haven Information

🌙 Overview

Moonlight Haven is a structured online community designed to bring people together through interaction, rewards, and services. The platform operates across Discord and WhatsApp, allowing members to connect, participate in events, and earn rewards through activity and engagement.

The ecosystem is powered by Moonlight Coins (MC), which act as the internal currency used to access various services and features within the community.

💎 Moonlight Coins (MC)

Moonlight Coins are the official digital currency of the Moonlight ecosystem. They are earned through participation, referrals, and events.

  • Earn MC by inviting new members
  • Earn MC through community participation
  • Receive MC from events and giveaways
  • Use MC to purchase services in the shop

MC transactions are managed automatically by the system bot to ensure fairness and consistency.

🎮 How to Participate

To get started in Moonlight Haven:

  • Join the Discord or WhatsApp community
  • Stay active and engage with other members
  • Participate in events and giveaways
  • Invite others to grow the community
  • Use earned MC in the shop for services

The more active you are, the more opportunities you have to earn rewards.

🛒 Shop System

The shop provides access to various services such as partnerships, promotions, custom roles, bot commands, and server features.

  • All items are priced in MC
  • Services are delivered after verification
  • Proof may be required for certain transactions
  • Requests are handled through the bot system

The shop is continuously updated with new services and features.

📜 Rules & Guidelines

All members are expected to follow community rules:

  • Respect all members and staff
  • No spam or misleading content
  • No exploitation of rewards or systems
  • No scams or fraudulent activity
  • Follow instructions given by moderators and bots

Violating rules may result in warnings, restrictions, or removal from the community.
